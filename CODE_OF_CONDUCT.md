# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful, constructive, and
welcoming for contributors, benchmark users, implementers, and reviewers.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific in technical disagreement.
- Assume good faith, but ground claims in evidence.
- Keep feedback focused on code, documentation, tests, reproducibility, and
  benchmark integrity.
- Respect the public/held-out boundary for benchmark material.
- Avoid sharing private prompts, private outputs, credentials, or customer data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private benchmark holdout content or credentials.
- Misrepresenting benchmark scores, release-contract state, or claim eligibility.
- Sustained disruption of issue, pull request, or review workflows.

## Enforcement

During handover, maintainers for the Agentsia-hosted repository may remove
comments, close issues, restrict participation, or escalate security-sensitive
material through the security process. After transfer, IAB Tech Lab is expected
to apply its own code-of-conduct and enforcement process.

When reporting conduct concerns, include links, screenshots, or other relevant
context and state whether the concern also involves private data or security
material.
